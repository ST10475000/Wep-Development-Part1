About The Plug Plumbers

Founded in 2017 as a family-run business, The Plug Plumbers began with a single van and a simple promise: show up on time, do the job right, and treat people fairly. Today, our teams operate across Johannesburg, serving homeowners, landlords, property managers and small businesses.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide fast, reliable and affordable plumbing solutions while maintaining exceptional customer service.

Vision: To become Gauteng’s most trusted plumbing brand, known for quality workmanship and excellent client experiences.

Our Values

  • Safety: We follow best practices and SANS guidelines on every job.
  • Honesty: Transparent quotes, no hidden fees.
  • Respect: We treat your home or business like our own.
  • Accountability: We stand behind our work—guaranteed.
